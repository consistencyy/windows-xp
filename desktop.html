Overview

Consistencyy is a creative persona and multidisciplinary artist known for vivid storytelling across motion graphics, visual design, and film. His works often blend nostalgia, glitch art, and emotional resonance to produce a unique digital language.

Career

Starting in independent animation and progressing into branded content, short films, and visual identities, Consistencyy has built a portfolio showcasing both artistic depth and strategic design thinking.

Style

Influenced by analog aesthetics, VHS textures, anime, and urban grit, his work often employs layered compositions, symbolic editing, and glitch-based rhythms to evoke emotional undercurrents.
