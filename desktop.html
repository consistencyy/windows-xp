About My Work

Design that ships.
Then gets installed.

I'm Conor — a 24-year-old graphic designer and production specialist based out of Westchester, New York. I got into this because I love making things that actually exist in the world, not just on a screen. My background spans brand identity, wide-format print, vinyl graphics, and on-site installation.

I studied Graphic Design at SUNY Buffalo with a minor in Film & Media Studies, which is probably why I think about storytelling as much as I think about bleeds. Outside of client work, I create motion graphics, edit videos, and apparently hand-code interactive Windows XP desktop environments for my portfolio. (Like this one!)

The digital space is home base. The real world is where the work lives.

Conor McCutcheon

Graphic Designer & Production Specialist · Notary Public
📍 Yonkers, New York · consistency.ink · conormcc.edits@gmail.com

Conor McCutcheon (b. 2001) — Yonkers-based graphic designer and production specialist, currently employed at FASTSIGNS of New Rochelle as Graphic Designer & Production Specialist. Experience spanning brand identity, wide-format print, vinyl plotting, lamination, and on-site installation. B.A. in Graphic Design from SUNY Buffalo (2023). Certified Notary Public, State of New York (2025–2029). FAA TRUST Drone Certified.

Career

FASTSIGNS of New Rochelle Current

2025 – Present  ·  Graphic Designer & Production Specialist

Full lifecycle of commercial print and sign production — from initial design through prepress, wide-format printing, lamination, and finish cutting on a vinyl plotter. Day-to-day responsibilities included adapting client artwork into production-ready files, performing nozzle checks and printer maintenance, vectorizing artwork, enforcing brand guidelines, and designing signage, wayfinding systems, logos, and branded materials from scratch.

  • Wide-format inkjet printer operation and daily maintenance (nozzle checks, troubleshooting)
  • Full print production workflow: file prep, color output, lamination, vinyl plotting, substrate finishing
  • Client file prepress — vectorizing artwork, color mode conversion, optimization for large-format output
  • Signage, wayfinding, logo, and brand identity design from scratch for diverse clients
  • Multi-project production management under tight turnaround deadlines

The UPS Store, Yonkers

Aug. 2024 – Jan. 2025  ·  Graphic Designer & Notary Public

Custom design and print projects for walk-in clients, including business cards, banners, and marketing materials. Provided notarial services for legal and real estate documents as a commissioned New York State Notary Public. Trained team members on design software and print equipment operation.

Freelance Graphic Designer & Video Editor

2023 – Present  ·  Independent · Westchester, NY

Brand identity, print collateral, wide-format production, and video across a range of clients and industries — from logo design and vinyl sign installation to social media campaigns and motion graphics. Selected clients include Allied Converters, Alpha Recycling, Cell Phone Repair Lab LLC, Exit Here LLC, Hair Lord, Jack's Bait & Tackle, Jewel-Art Inc., Librett Real Estate Group, and Liberty Mason Work, among others. Maintains personal portfolio site (consistency.ink) — a hand-coded interactive Windows XP desktop environment built in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
