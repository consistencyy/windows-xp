Conor McCutcheon

Graphic Designer · Production Specialist · Notary Public
📍 Yonkers, New York · conor.style · conormcc.edits@gmail.com

Conor McCutcheon (b. 2001) — Yonkers-based graphic designer and production specialist, currently at FASTSIGNS of New Rochelle. I work across wide-format print, vinyl plotting, lamination, and brand design. BA in Graphic Design from SUNY Buffalo (2023). Certified Notary Public, State of New York.

ℹ️  For his video work, see Consistencyy (YouTube channel). For portfolio projects, see Projects (My Files).

Career

FASTSIGNS of New Rochelle Current

2025 – Present  ·  Graphic Designer & Production Assistant

Full lifecycle of commercial print — from design in Illustrator and Photoshop through prepress, printing, lamination, and finish cutting on a Roland vinyl plotter. Substrates include vinyl, foam board, banner material, and rigid aluminum composites. Day-to-day: adapting client artwork into production-ready files, color-correcting for large-format output, and managing my own brand design projects on the side.

  • Wide-format printing, lamination, and vinyl plotter operation (Roland series)
  • Prepress preparation: bleed setup, color mode conversion, file optimization for print
  • Vehicle wrap design, window graphics, and ADA-compliant signage
  • Client-facing design consultations and brand adaptation for print media
  • Managing personal design projects and brand identity work alongside production duties

PopGeeks.com

Aug. 2023 – Present  ·  Production Assistant & Designer

Multimedia pop culture outlet. Designed branded content, maintained the site in HTML/CSS, and covered live events on DSLR. Full post-production: editing, color grading, graphics — for both editorial and social.

  • Branded content design: business cards, blog headers, social media assets
  • HTML/CSS site updates for multimedia articles and event coverage
  • DSLR event photography and video, full post-production workflow

Freelance Design & Video

2021 – Present

Branding, print collateral, social campaigns, and promo video across a range of clients and industries. I handle everything from brief to delivery.
